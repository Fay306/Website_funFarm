Park

Near our Zoo section, there is a bueatiful park, where people can people go in order to relax and enjoy themselves. Deep in side of the park, there is a lake, people can sit on the grass ground and have a BBQ or relax time. There are quite a lot of difference trees and plants in the park, so people can have a real fresh air in the park and without any city noise.

In addition, our park is an area of natural and planted space set aside for human enjoyment and recreation. In the park, people might lucky to see some wild animals, for example, deers, squirrels, wild rabbits and fox ext.. More, some people like to do the exercises in the park, because there will make them calm down and release the stress. You can go there with your parents, kids, friends or partner.
